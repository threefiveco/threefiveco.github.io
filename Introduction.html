Introduction Page

Hey Alle. Welcome to alle.space! This website is a dedication to you. It is my way of helping you out with things that you might want me to help you out in the first place. There are three reasons for me to do this:

  1. I love you.
  2. I can practice my web developing skills.
  3. I improve my skills (writing, habits, et al) by laying them out on a website.

There are a bunch of things you can do here. Anything from learning how to parallel park to shooting me a DM in case you need a favor or want me to add something else. It's also a way for you to satisfy the craving of texting me or trying to come in direct contact with me. I will consistently try to update this website whenever I have a chance, but if I don't for a while it doesn't mean that I forgot about you. It just means that I've been busy or that I am being lazy because believe it or not it can be a tedious process. But trust me there will always be something new on here. I might even hide a couple of things around so that you have to go on a scavenger hunt! This bit of text is just a sort of introduction to this place to kind of get an idea of what you can expect from it.

Finally I just want to say once again for the billionth time how much I care about you. It's very important for us to do this so that we can become the best versions of ourselves before we settle down. I am already doing a bunch of things to get my life in order. I'm thinking about doing a sort of monthly diary here kind of writing about what I've achieved each month, but I don't know if that's a good idea for us. I'd like to hear your opinion on that. Anyway, I love you more than life itself and I hope you like this website.

P.S. I know the list is supposed to be indented. I'm working on it.
